# Good Practices for Software Development

## Question : 1 Which point(s) were new to you?

Explaining problems clearly with what I already tried before asking for help.  

## Question : 2 Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?

Area to Improve: Communication and asking better technical questions.

Ideas to Improve:
Clearly explain the problem with error messages and screenshots.  
Mention the solutions I already tried.  
Prepare questions before meetings.  
Avoid sending incomplete or unclear doubts.  
Practice structured communication in team discussions.
