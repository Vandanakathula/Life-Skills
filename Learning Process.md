# Learning-Process
## 1. How to Learn Faster with the Feynman Technique

### 1. What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.
The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you deeply understand a concept by explaining it in simple terms as if teaching someone else.

## 2. Learning How to Learn TED talk by Barbara Oakley.

### 2. In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?
The most interesting idea was how Barbara Oakley changed herself from someone who struggled and failed in math and science to becoming an engineering professor by learning how to study effectively and using focused and diffuse thinking modes.

### 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
Active (focused) mode is when we concentrate hard on solving a problem or learning something familiar step by step, while diffuse mode happens when we relax or take a break, allowing the brain to make wider connections and help solve difficult or new problems.

## 3. Learn Anything in 20 hours.
### 4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.
Deconstruct the skill into smaller parts.

Learn enough to self-correct.

Remove barriers and distractions to practice.

Practice the skill for at least 20 hours.

## 4. Learning Principles in the Bootcamp
### 5. What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?
Maintain full focus and avoid distractions while learning or coding.

Block social media and silence notifications during work hours.

Focus on understanding concepts and implementing them in code.

Practice regularly and complete assignments within planned timelines.

Communicate proactively when facing difficulties and clarify doubts.
