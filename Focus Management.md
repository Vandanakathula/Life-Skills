# Focus Management

## 1. What is Deep Work

### Question 1 : What is Deep Work ?

Deep Work is a concept explained by Cal Newport.

Deep Work means doing one important task with full focus and no distractions.  
It means not using your phone, not checking social media, and not switching between tasks.

When we do deep work, we understand things better and complete work with high quality.

In simple words, Deep Work is working with full concentration on one task.

## 4. Summary of Deep Work Book
 
### Question 2 : According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

- Work without distractions (no phone, no social media).  
- Focus on one task at a time.  
- Set a fixed time for deep work.  
- Take small breaks after focused sessions.  
- Practice it daily to improve concentration.

### Question 3 : How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- Keep your phone away while studying or working.  
- Plan your day and fix time for important tasks.  
- Avoid multitasking.  
- Reduce unnecessary social media usage.  
- Create a quiet place to work.

## 5. Dangers of Social Media (Brief)

### Question 4 : What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- Wastes a lot of time.  
- Reduces concentration.  
- Creates addiction.  
- Increases stress and comparison with others.  
- Distracts us from important goals.
