## 4. Sleep is your Superpower

### Question 4: Paraphrase the video in brief (only points)

- Sleep improves memory and learning ability  
- It strengthens the immune system  
- Sleep reduces stress and emotional imbalance  
- Lack of sleep affects mental and physical health  
- Deep sleep helps the brain and body recover  
- Sleep improves focus and decision-making  

### Question 5: What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?

- Sleep at the same time every day  
- Avoid mobile/TV before bed  
- Reduce caffeine intake  
- Keep the room dark and cool  
- Avoid late-night heavy meals  

## 5. Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise

### Question 6: Paraphrase the video (minimum 5 points)

- Exercise improves brain function  
- It increases focus and attention  
- Physical activity reduces stress and anxiety  
- Exercise improves memory  
- It helps in long-term brain health  
- Regular exercise boosts mood  

### Question 7: What are some steps you can take to exercise more?

- Walk daily for at least 20–30 minutes  
- Do simple home workouts  
- Stretch regularly  
- Join a gym or fitness class  
- Set a fixed time for exercise
